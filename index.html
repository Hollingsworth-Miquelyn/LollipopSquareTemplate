about the author

Ashley Christensen

I guess you could say my love for Children’s books comes from being raised in a family where bedtime, was the best time. Each night without fail, my dad would find his spot on our bedroom floor and off we'd go on his story adventures. I could picture the characters so clearly in my mind, that many of them have found their way into the stories I tell my own littles some 30 years later.

For me, it wasn't just about the story or the characters, it was about the time we spent creating them.

My mom, the writer and artist in the family, brought wisdom, inspiration, creativity, fun and goodness into our home by sharing her talents through pen and paintbrush. I only hope I can follow in her footsteps.

I have been writing stories my whole life. Some of the characters in these books have been waiting decades to make their appearance in the world. Welcome friends, finally!

I write for little children, mostly because, I live in a little kid world. As the mother of three, I am blessed to have the most hilarious real-life inspiration for my characters and their wild adventures. My little ones bring laughter into our home minute by minute, along with a lot of mud, crumbs and songs that are sung incredibly louder than necessary. But, I’m happy. There’s love, and lots of it.

I have to also give a fist pump to my husband Mike. He is the real brawn and brains of this operation. And truly, the bedtime stories he tells to our children take me back to my childhood and remind me why all of this is so important.

So THANK YOU for allowing our stories into your life.

It is our commitment to offer you and your littles happy and wholesome stories that can create lasting memories for years to come... And who knows, maybe you'll be inspired to write a few of your own!
